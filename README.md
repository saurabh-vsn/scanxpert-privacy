# ScanXpert Privacy Policy

_Last updated: November 9, 2025_

Welcome to ScanXpert!  
Your privacy is very important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how ScanXpert collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our mobile app.

---

## 1. Information Collection

### a. Personal Information
- ScanXpert **does not request or collect personal information** such as your name, email, phone number, or address.

### b. Usage Data
- The app may collect anonymous usage statistics to help improve features and user experience. No usage data is used to identify you personally.

### c. Permissions
ScanXpert may request the following device permissions:
- **Storage Access:** To select, create, and save PDF files on your device.
- **Camera:** If you use “Image to PDF” or scanning features, only for capturing images—no photos or data are sent anywhere.
- **Internet:** (Optional, only for updates and bug reporting, NOT for transmitting your documents.)

We do **not** access or collect any files, photos, or data outside the specific documents you choose to work with.

---

## 2. Data Usage

- **Local Processing:** All PDF actions (merge, split, sign, annotate, convert images) are performed **locally** on your device.
- Your files and documents never leave your phone or tablet unless you explicitly share them.
- No document content, text, or images are transmitted to our servers or any third party.

---

## 3. Data Sharing

- **We do not share, sell, or disclose any data to third parties.**
- The app does not use advertising or third-party analytics SDKs.

---

## 4. Data Security

- ScanXpert uses the security features of your device and Android OS.
- Documents processed are stored only in app-specific folders, unless you choose other locations.
- If you protect a PDF with a password, it is encrypted using standard PDF encryption methods.

---

## 5. Children’s Privacy

- ScanXpert is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

- The app may use trusted open-source libraries (for PDF handling) that do **not** transmit user data.
- App stores (Google Play) may collect anonymous usage info for app analytics.

---

## 7. Changes to Policy

- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and you should check regularly for updates.

---

By using ScanXpert, you agree to this privacy policy.

Thank you for trusting us with your document needs!
